Stock Trading App

The "Stock Trade" project involved creating a platform for users to engage in buying and selling stocks, with functionalities catering to both customers and administrators. Customers could manage their portfolios, execute market and limit orders, and handle cash transactions. Administrators had the ability to set up new stocks and manage market operations. The system was designed for concurrent multi-user access, backed by a relational database, and featured a GUI for interaction. The project emphasized real-time stock price fluctuations and included comprehensive documentation, a demo video, and deployment instructions.
